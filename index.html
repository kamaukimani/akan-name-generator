ABOUT THE WEBSITE

Akan Name Generator is a simple, interactive website designed to help users discover their traditional Akan name based on their date of birth and gender.
In Akan culture, names are deeply meaningful and are given according to the day of the week a person is born.
This site provides an easy and user-friendly experience that allows anyone regardless of background or location to explore and connect with this unique aspect of Ghanaian heritage.
Whether you're curious about your cultural roots or just want to try something new, this tool offers a fun and educational way to learn more about Akan naming traditions.
